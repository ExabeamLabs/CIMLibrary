 Platforms by Landscapes
========================

In the Common Information Model, platforms identify the virtual environments or applications in which events occur (such as Windows, Okta, O365, GitHub). Landscapes identify the umbrella categories for the platforms (such as cloud, endpoint, database, vpn).
